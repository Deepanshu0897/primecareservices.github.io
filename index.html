Who We Are

Rooted in Healthcare.
Built on Trust.

Prime Care Services was founded as a specialized locum staffing agency for long-term care homes across BC. That clinical foundation — RNs, LPNs, and certified HCAs — is now brought directly into the homes of seniors and families who deserve more than standard care.

🏥
Clinical-Grade Professionals Our team includes Registered Nurses, Licensed Practical Nurses, and certified Health Care Assistants.
🛡️
Fully Screened & Insured Every caregiver holds a Vulnerable Sector Check and is covered under Prime Care's comprehensive insurance.
🌏
Multilingual & Culturally Aware Punjabi-speaking staff and a team that reflects and respects the rich diversity of Abbotsford's community.
What We Offer

Two Core Services.
One Trusted Team.

Every service Prime Care provides is built around one goal — helping your loved one remain safely and comfortably at home, for as long as possible.
01
🏠

In-Home Care

Comprehensive daily support including personal care, bathing, dressing, grooming, medication management, meal preparation, and mobility assistance — all in the comfort of home.

02
🫶

Companion Care

Meaningful social visits, recreational engagement, accompaniment to appointments, and emotional support — because connection is just as essential as physical care.

03
😴

Respite Care

Temporary, high-quality relief for family caregivers. We step in so you can rest and recharge, confident your loved one is in expert hands.

04
💊

Medication Management

Trained caregivers handle medication reminders, dosage tracking, and prescription coordination with clinical precision.

05
🩺

Post-Surgery Recovery

Skilled in-home support following hospital discharge — ensuring a safe, smooth recovery with professional monitoring every step of the way.

Getting Started

From First Call to First Visit in Four Steps

Starting care should never feel overwhelming. We have made the process simple, transparent, and built entirely around your family's timeline — not ours.

01
↗

Free Consultation

Call, email, or fill out our form. We arrange a no-pressure conversation at your convenience — by phone or in person in Abbotsford.

02
↗

In-Home Assessment

We visit your loved one's home, document their care needs, safety considerations, and personal preferences in a detailed care plan.

03
↗

Caregiver Matching

We carefully match your loved one with the right caregiver — considering skills, language, schedule, and personal compatibility.

04
↗

Care Begins

Care starts on your timeline. We check in with your family regularly and adjust the care plan as your loved one's needs evolve.
